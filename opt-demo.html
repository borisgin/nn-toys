Training Linear Network with NovoGrad

This is the companion demo for "Stochastic Gradient Methods with Layer-wise Adaptive Moments for Training of Deep Networks"

We will train network: y = w2 * w1 * x . The network is composed from two linear layers, and it should output 1 when input is 1. This is equivalent to the minimization of function y = (w2*w1 - 1)2 . The function is non-convex, with minima located on hypebola w2 = 1 / w1 . Solutions located near (-1,-1) and (1,1) are good flat minima, which generalize well, and solutions near axes are bad "sharp" minima.

We will use 4 optimizers: SGD with momentum, Adam, AdamW, and NovoGrad to minimize the function.
